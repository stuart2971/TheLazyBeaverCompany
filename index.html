HOW WE MADE IT HAPPEN

The reason why our systems do not require energy

Our ‘smart valve’ allows total reduction of the fluid level before it refills. When connected to a water supply, the valve allows water to enter the bottom of the pot to a certain depth.  The valve then closes and will not allow more water to enter the container until all the given water has been used up.  Once the water has been absorbed, the valve re-opens and more water enters the growing container, providing wet-dry cycles dictated by the plant itself
