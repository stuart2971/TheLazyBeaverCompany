hydropak Starter kit

No pumps. No power. No problems.

Complete care-free Hydroponic system. Everything you need, except the seed.

Each professional kit includes everything you need to grow immediately:
‍
- 35 litre reservoir
- 2 x 10” pots and double tray
- Patented Smart-Valve and cover
- Tubing, fittings, mats and filter
- Perlite / Vermiculite (50/50 mix)
- Nutrients 1 litre of A and B
- Instruction booklet

Effective. Reliable. Maintenance free.

The Hydroponic starter kit allows beginners and expert gardeners the ability to grow and experience professional results without the complications or complexities of conventional hydroponic systems.
Simply plant seedlings or seeds, fill the tank with water, add a measure of nutrient and enjoy the spectacular return of pleasure and produce. It truly is that easy.
The patented Smart-Valve technology, makes the Lazy Beaver hydroponic systems versatile, effective and inexpensive to run. It’s the ideal way to grow with minimal attention.

Simple Instructions for set up:
‍
1. Lift lid and remove contents from 35 litre reservoir
2. Place mat in bottom of pots. Place growing medium in pots.
3. Saturate with water / nutrient dilution and drain to remove dust.
4. Place Smart-Valve into tray.
5. Connect tubing to reservoir and filter. Connect tubing to filter and Smart-Valve.
6. Fill reservoir with water and add measured nutrients A and B.
7. Place pots into tray.
8. Cover Smart-Valve. Place lid on reservoir.
9. Add seed or seedling.
10. Wait for outstanding results.

All Lazy Beaver kits can also be extended to add more growing points.
