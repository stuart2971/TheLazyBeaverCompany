Window box kit

​For beginners who prefers growing herbs, ornamentals and smaller varieties, the Window Box Kit is for you...
‍
Simple to use:
*Plant the seedlings in to the 6" pots with normal potting mix, perlite/vermiculite mix or combination of both.
* Position the pots in to the slots of the Window Box unit.
* Fill up the reservoir tank with water and nutrients.
* Thereafter, the Smart-valve will know exactly how to water and feed your plants correctly.
* You can grow practically any shallow rooting plants successfully including Cyclamens, Violas, Pansies, Labellias, Bridal veil, Petunia etc.​

The Window Box kit contains the following items
* 1 x Window box trough (Size: 800mm x 180mmx 120mm)
* 1 x Hydrotank with 4mm grommet - 35L
* 1 x Smart-valve
* 4 x 6" pots with Marix root control mats
* 1 x roll of 4mm tubing (2m)
* 1 x 4mm filter
* 2 x 4mm taps
* 1 x pair of 1L A/B Nutrients
* 1 x 5L bag of perlite/vermiculite mix
* 1 x Instruction booklet
