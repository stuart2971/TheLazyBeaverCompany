Mulch Mats

Mats for 10" or 12" pots.

Used as
-an inorganic mulch for keeping the growing medium cool in summer and warm in winter.
-Preventing algae growing on top of the perlite.
- Reducing water loss through surface evaporation.
- Minimizing salt build up.
