Liquid nutrients


Over the years, we have perfected our nutrient formulation specially suited for use. Unlike most standard hydroponics nutrients, we adopt one formulation for both grow and bloom.Available as 1L, 2.5L, 5L, and 10L pairs (A & B).+

Major components are:
Nitrogen
Potassium
Phosphorus
Calcium
Magnesium
Minor (trace elements):
Iron
Manganese
Copper
Boron
Molybdenum
Zinc

Laznutrients are different from common garden fertilizers in at least 5 important ways.
‍
1) they are 100% water soluble
2) They come in a form that is immediately usable by the plant
3) They are a much higher degree of purity, being manufactured to a technical grade.
4) They are more expensive than common garden fertilizers
5) They generally come in a two part mix - A & B.

A carries all the phosphate and potash ingredients and trace elements essential in the development of flower, fruit and produce growth.B carries the calcium and nitrogen. Roots, leaf and overall growth or the plant.
Available as 500gm, 1kg, 5kg and 25kg pairs (A & B).

All our plants are fed with only one nutrient mix without the need for having separate "Grow" and "Bloom" formulations.In hydroponics when we speak of nutrients in the food supply for the plant we speak directly to the entire food chain. The media makes no contribution to the health, welfare or general nurture of the plant, other than stabilization. Everything the plant needs, other than light, warmth and location must be delivered through the nutrient solution.
