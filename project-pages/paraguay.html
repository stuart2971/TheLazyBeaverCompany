Paraguay

Juan researched the internet for a hydroponic system that is simple and applicable for his sub tropical climatic conditions. After thorough investigation and a trip to visit to our systems in Melbourne, they were convinced that our system is the most suitable system for him in Paraguay. In order to reduce the shipping costs, Juan had to substitute the pots with plastic bags as the growing containers. He used blue stone chips for the growing medium. He has no previous experience in hydroponics and yet produced excellent results with his first attempt – as shown in the pictures below.
