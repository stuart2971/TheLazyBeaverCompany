Pulau Manis Farm

Project Summary

Size: 20 acres.
50 resident homes.
Given to the poor by IRIS Corporation.
In 2010, the Prime Minister of Malaysia, Datuk Seri Najib, handed over the keys.
19 greenhouses were built to generate economic activities for the settlement.
Total of 30,000 units for the planting of high value crops eg. Melons.
A beautiful traditional Malay house built for homestay services.
New police station for the community.
